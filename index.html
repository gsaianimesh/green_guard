Smart Verification Features üõ°Ô∏è

Built-in safeguards ensure your care activities are authentic and help maintain trust in our community

gps_fixed

Location Verification

When you log tree care, we verify that you're actually at the tree location. Your photo's GPS coordinates must be within 50 meters of the registered tree location.

check_circle Automatically verified from your photo
schedule

Fair Care Distribution

To ensure everyone gets a chance to care for trees, you can only log care for the same tree once every 24 hours. This prevents duplicate entries and encourages community participation.

check_circle One care log per tree per day
photo_camera

Photo Proof Required

Every care activity requires a photo taken at the time of care. The photo must be taken within 15 minutes of logging the activity, and must include location data to verify authenticity.

check_circle Real-time verification from your camera
security

Quality Protection

Our system automatically detects unusual patterns like rapid multiple logs or location mismatches. These activities are flagged for review by our admin team to ensure data quality and prevent misuse.

check_circle Admin-reviewed for accuracy
