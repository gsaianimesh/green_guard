About GreenGuard

A rule-based, community-driven Green Tech platform designed to improve tree survival rates through long-term care, accountability, and community ownership.

public

üåç Overview

The Tree Care & Community Platform is a rule-based, community-driven Green Tech application designed to improve tree survival rates after plantation. Instead of focusing only on planting trees, the platform emphasizes long-term care, accountability, and community ownership.

The system enables individuals, schools, NGOs, and corporates to collaboratively monitor, care for, and sustain trees using transparent logic-based technology ‚Äî without using AI or machine learning.

Rule-Based Monitoring & Validation (NO AI)

The system uses logical rules, not AI, to ensure authenticity. This includes distance validation, time-based restrictions, manual health checklists, and deterministic health scoring formulas. This ensures fairness, transparency, and full compliance with non-AI constraints.
